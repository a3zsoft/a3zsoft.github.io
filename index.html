Our Core Principles

App Navigation & Key Areas

Gro is organized into several main sections (Index Tabs) and key management areas to help you easily find what you need:

1. Money Tab

This is your daily financial hub for tracking transactions, managing accounts, and viewing immediate insights.

Insights Overview:

  • Savings View: See your net savings (Income - Expenses) for the selected period, with a clear indication of whether you're in a positive, negative, or break-even state. Includes quick links to analyze savings further.
  • Expenses View: Get a breakdown of your expenses by category. See total expenses and compare against category budgets (if set for the month). Includes quick links to analyze expenses and need vs. want spending.
  • Income View: Understand your income sources, broken down by category. Track total income and compare against income targets (if set for the month). Includes quick links to analyze income and active vs. passive income.
  • Transfers View: Review your transfer history between accounts for the selected period. Easily initiate new transfers.

Transactions List:

  • View a detailed list of all your transactions.
  • Filter & Sort: Filter by account(s), period (day, week, month, year, custom range), type (all, expenses, income), or specific category. Sort by date or amount.
  • Search: Quickly find transactions by comment, amount, date, category name, or account name.
  • Add/Edit Transactions: Use the innovative draggable Floating Action Button (FAB): drag left for expense, drag right for income. Tap the FAB for a standard transaction entry form. Tap any transaction in the list to edit or delete it.

Account Management (via Account Selector & Grid Menu):

  • Select single or multiple accounts to focus your view using the Account Selector in the app bar. A "Total" view aggregates all accounts.
  • Add, edit, and manage details for all your financial accounts (bank, cash, credit cards, etc.).

Grid Menu Options (accessible from the Money Tab):

  • Categories: Access and manage your transaction categories.
  • Transfers: Initiate and view inter-account transfers.
  • Repeat Transactions: Set up and manage recurring transactions.
  • Import Transactions: Import transaction data from external files.
  • Quick Start Guide & Tutorial: A helpful guide for new users to get acquainted with the Money tab's features, along with an interactive tutorial for key functionalities.

2. Plans Tab

This section is dedicated to your long-term financial planning and strategy. It helps you organize and track your progress towards significant financial milestones.

  • Budget & Targets: Your financial command center for setting spending limits (Expense budgets) or income goals (Income targets). Define them monthly or yearly, link transaction categories, and track progress.
  • Assets & Net Worth: Understand what you own and its value. Track real estate, vehicles, investments, collectibles, and other assets. Your 'All Bank Accounts' asset is auto-calculated. This section is crucial for understanding your Net Worth (Assets - Liabilities).
  • Debts & Liabilities: Get a clear picture of everything you owe. Manage loans (car, student, mortgage), installment plans, and informal IOUs. Track progress, remaining balances, and view amortization schedules.
  • Financial Independence (FIRE): Plan for early retirement or financial freedom. Calculate your 'FIRE Number' (amount needed to live off investments) and project when you might reach it based on your financial inputs.

Each section within the Plans tab provides detailed "How to Use" and "Terminology Explained" guides accessible directly within the app via the Plan Info Dialog.

3. Analytics Tab

Dive deeper into your financial data with advanced analytics and visualizations. This section helps you identify trends, understand complex patterns, and gain more profound insights into your financial behavior.

  • Expense Analysis: Detailed breakdown of expenses, including analysis of "Need vs. Want" spending.
  • Income Analysis: In-depth look at your income streams, including analysis of "Active vs. Passive" income.
  • Savings & Investments Analysis: Track your savings rate, investment growth, and progress towards financial goals.

General App Features:

  • Theme Support: Gro supports both Light and Dark themes, adapting to your system settings or allowing manual selection for comfortable viewing in any environment.
  • Multi-Currency Support: Manage and track your finances across different currencies, with options to set a primary currency and view converted values.

Detailed Core Features Explanation

Transaction Tracking (Expense/Income)

At the heart of Gro is its robust transaction tracking. You can effortlessly log every expense and income entry.

  • Input Methods: Use the intuitive draggable Floating Action Button (FAB) on the Money tab – drag left for expenses, right for income – for super-fast entries. Alternatively, tap the FAB for a detailed dialog.
  • Details: Record amount, date, select a relevant category, assign it to an account, and add descriptive comments or tags.
  • Editing: Easily modify or delete any past transaction directly from the transaction list.

Account Management

Gro allows you to consolidate all your financial accounts in one place for a complete overview.

  • Creation & Types: Add various types of accounts, such as bank accounts (checking, savings), credit cards, cash, investment accounts, and more.
  • Balance Tracking: Account balances are automatically updated as you log transactions linked to them. The 'All Bank Accounts' asset in the Plans tab auto-calculates the sum of your bank-type accounts.
  • Customization: Assign names, starting balances, icons, and colors to your accounts for easy identification.
  • Filtering: Use the account selector in the Money tab to view transactions and insights for specific accounts or a combined total.

Category Management

Organize your financial life with a flexible category system.

  • Default & Custom: Gro comes with a set of default income and expense categories, but you have full control to customize them.
  • Operations: Add new categories, edit existing ones (name, icon, color), or delete those you don't need. This is typically managed via the "Categories" option in the Money tab's grid menu.
  • Budget Linking: Categories can be directly linked to your budgets and targets in the Plans tab, and you can even set specific monthly budget amounts per category.

Budgeting & Goal Setting

Take control of your spending and work towards your financial goals with Gro's planning tools.

  • Budgets (Expenses): In the "Plans" tab, create budgets for various expense categories (e.g., Groceries, Entertainment). Set monthly or yearly limits.
  • Targets (Income): Similarly, set income targets to motivate and track your earnings.
  • Progress Tracking: Gro visually shows your progress against these budgets/targets, highlighting how much you've spent/earned and what's remaining.

Asset & Debt Tracking

Understand your complete financial picture by tracking what you own (assets) and what you owe (liabilities).

  • Assets: In the "Plans" tab, list and monitor the value of your assets like real estate, vehicles, investments, and other valuables. The 'All Bank Accounts' asset is automatically updated.
  • Debts: Manage loans (mortgages, student loans, car loans) and other liabilities. Track original amounts, interest rates (APR), terms, and payment progress. View amortization schedules to see how payments are allocated.

Net Worth Conceptualization

Gro helps you understand and build towards a positive net worth.

  • Calculation: While a dedicated "Net Worth" page might consolidate this, the "Assets & Net Worth" and "Debts & Liabilities" sections in the "Plans" tab provide the core components (Assets - Liabilities = Net Worth).
  • Tracking: By keeping your assets and debts updated, you can monitor how your net worth changes over time.

FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) Planning

For those aiming for early financial freedom, Gro offers a dedicated FIRE planning tool in the "Plans" tab.

  • FIRE Number Calculation: Input your financial details (or use app-calculated averages for expenses/income), expected inflation, savings rate, withdrawal rate, and anticipated income growth. Gro calculates your target "FIRE Number."
  • Projections: View estimated timelines to reach your FIRE goal and projections for both accumulation and withdrawal phases.

Financial Insights & Reporting

Gro transforms raw data into meaningful information.

  • Money Tab Insights: Get quick daily/weekly/monthly summaries of your savings, expenses (by category), and income (by source).
  • Analytics Tab: Dive deeper with more comprehensive reports and visualizations. Analyze spending trends (e.g., "Need vs. Want"), income patterns (e.g., "Active vs. Passive"), and savings performance over time.

Data Filtering & Searching

Easily find the information you need within your transaction history.

  • Filtering: In the "Money" tab, filter transactions by date period (day, week, month, year, custom range), by specific account(s), by transaction type (expense, income), or by tapping on a category in the insights view.
  • Sorting: Sort your transaction list by date (ascending/descending) or amount (ascending/descending).
  • Searching: Use the search bar to quickly locate transactions based on keywords in comments, amounts, dates, category names, or account names.

Tutorials & Guides

Gro is designed to be user-friendly, with help available when you need it.

  • Quick Start Guide: The "Money" tab often includes a Quick Start Guide to help new users get familiar with its main features.
  • Interactive Tutorials: Contextual tutorials (like the one for the Money tab) guide you through key functionalities using highlighted overlays.
  • Plan Info Dialogs: Each module within the "Plans" tab (Budget, Assets, Debts, FIRE) has its own detailed information dialog, explaining its purpose, how to use it, and relevant terminology.

How to Use Gro

Getting started and using Gro effectively is straightforward. Here’s a general guide:

Getting Started:

Initial Setup:

  • When you first open the app, you might be greeted by a tutorial or a Quick Start Guide, especially in the "Money" section. Follow these prompts.
  • Add Your Accounts: Navigate to account management (often via the Account Selector in the "Money" tab's app bar or a dedicated section in settings/grid menu). Add your bank accounts, credit cards, cash, etc. This is crucial for tracking.
  • Customize Categories: Access "Categories" (usually from the "Money" tab's grid menu). Review the default expense and income categories. Add, edit, or delete them to fit your spending and earning patterns. Assign custom icons and colors.

Set Preferences:

  • Explore the app settings (often via a settings icon) to choose your preferred theme (Light/Dark) and set your primary currency.

Daily & Regular Use (Money Tab):

  • Log Transactions: Use the Floating ActionButton (FAB) at the bottom of the "Money" tab. Drag Left for expense, Drag Right for income. Tap for the full dialog. Be consistent with logging for accurate records.
  • Review Insights: Regularly check the "Savings," "Expenses," and "Income" views. Use the period filter. Tap on categories or total cards in insights to filter the transaction list.
  • Manage Transfers: Use the "Transfers" feature (from the grid menu or insights view) to record money movements between your accounts.
  • Manage Repeating Transactions: If you have regular bills or income, set them up as "Repeat Transactions" (via the grid menu) to automate logging.

Financial Planning (Plans Tab):

  • Set Budgets & Targets: Go to "Budget & Targets," tap '+', define details, and link categories.
  • Track Assets & Net Worth: Go to "Assets & Net Worth," tap '+' to add assets. Update values periodically.
  • Manage Debts & Liabilities: Go to "Debts & Liabilities," tap '+' to add loans. Review schedules.
  • Explore FIRE: Go to "Financial Independence (FIRE)," tap edit (✏️) to input details, and view projections.

Utilizing App Features:

  • Account Selector: Filter views for specific accounts in the "Money" tab.
  • Grid Menu (Money Tab): Access Categories, Transfers, Repeat Transactions, Import Transactions.
  • Import Data: Use "Import Transactions" if you have data from other sources.
  • Settings Menu: Configure app preferences like theme, currency, and tutorial visibility.
  • Plan Info Dialogs: For detailed help within each "Plans" tab module, look for an info icon (i or ?).
